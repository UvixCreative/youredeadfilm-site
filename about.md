# About

IF YOU CAN READ THIS, YOU'RE DEAD is an indie short film by Ben Rankin. It's an absurdist dramedy about a man who wakes up to the newspaper headline:

<h1 style="text-align: center;"> IF YOU CAN READ THIS, YOU'RE DEAD</h1>

We want to share the creation process with YOU! You can be a part of this movie - either in front of the camera or behind it - by applying at our [casting call](/casting-call)! You can also support us by either [donating](https://www.gofundme.com/f/help-ben-rankin-make-his-first-short-film) or just following our socials!
